\entry*[ICML 2025]%
	\textbf{Discrepancies are Virtue: Weak-to-Strong Generalization through Lens of Intrinsic Dimension}\\
	Research project at NYU (advised by Prof. Qi Lei and Prof. Jason D. Lee), Aug 2024 -- Jan 2025.\\
	arXiv: \url{https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.05075} \quad GitHub: \url{https://github.com/Marcliyc/weak-to-strong}\\
	Designed and analyzed a statistical framework linking weak-to-strong generalization scaling laws to intrinsic/correlation dimensions, predicting performance with $<3\%$ error across parameter and sample-size scaling.\\
	Built a PyTorch/Transformers/Timm framework spanning 6 datasets and about 30 teacher--student pairs (LLaMA, Qwen, Swin, etc.) on vision and language tasks.\\
	Designed an algorithm to estimate correlation dimensions between billion-parameter models, reducing memory usage by six orders of magnitude.
